Nice to meet you

I’m Yinka Adesesan and I am Business designer. I help organisations understand contexts in order to create products, services and ventures with clarity. I have just concluded a Masters Diploma in Design Management from Instituto Europeo di Design Barcelona (where I focused on Business Design and Design Strategy).

Prior, I’ve had a meandering career that has spanned quite a number of domains. From Electrical Engineering (what I was trained in) and technology education, to founding and running a graphic and web development agency called Creovativ for 5 years.

I’m currently looking for opportunities in business design, design strategy digital transformation and user experience. Have something in mind? Get in touch!

In my evenings, I’m finding ways to help self-taught Nigerian designers level up their design skills using initiatives like LogoMarch. On weekends, I read hard science fiction and take long walks by the beach.

ON MY MIND — tech entrepreneurship, personal productivity, no-code tools, science fiction, futurism, design education.
