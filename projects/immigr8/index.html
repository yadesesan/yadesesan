project

Immigr8: Improving the journey of Migrant workers to Canada using design thinking

  • Business design
 back

Summary

In our globalized world there is increased economic migration to high income countries like Canada. Economic migrants face the problem of finding relevant information without wasting precious time. The concept of a crowdsourced AI platform was proposed as a solution - by providing personalized information based on the user’s unique profile. A multi-sided business model was designed with big picture numbers showing profitability in 3 years.

Challenge

To explore and identity opportunities for innovation along the Migrant worker’s immigration journey, to ideate and evaluate solutions and to create a strategy for the implementation of the tested strategy.

Process

This project was developed using the business design methodology. A way of working that applies the methods of design thinking for insight generation as well as lean thinking’s build - measure - learn cycle to prototype and validate elements of the business model.

Discover:

Users: The following user personas and customer journey were developed after analysing the data acquired from in-depth interviews exploring the journeys of users within the target market and supported by secondary data sources.

Insights:

Define: Reframing the problem

How might we provide personalized information and support from one source to our users as they go through the Express Entry immigration journey in a time saving and affordable way?

Develop: My solution concept

Click here to see a Video Prototype of Immigr8

Deliver: Prototyping and testing

The key assumptions regarding the problem / solution fit was tested using in depth exploratory interviews.

  • Users were surprised when they calculated the average amount of time they had spent just gathering information and were interested in reducing that time
  • Users confirmed that they struggled with finding information
  • Users confirmed that the solution concept which curated information on the process would be valuable

Conclusion

This project was a very challenging but enlightening journey; one that has seen me grow tremendously in my understanding of design tools and methods as well as sharpen the creative and innovative mindset needed to solve business problems in meaningful and value filled ways.
