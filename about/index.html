My name is Yinka Adesesan; originally from Abuja, Nigeria but now living in Barcelona, Spain. I help individuals, startups and companies creatively solve problems by leading the transformation of ideas into profitable products, services and ventures.

I have a Masters in Design Management from IED Barcelona with a focus in Design Strategy and digital transformation. Previous to that I founded and ran a small logo and web design agency called Creovativ for over seven years.

I love reading books, productivity hacks and making super fluffy pancakes.
